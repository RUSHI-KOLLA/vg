SUBHASH CHANDRA BOSE: THE INDIAN STRUGGLE AND SPEECHES

ON UNCONVENTIONAL ALLIANCES:
It is our duty to pay for our liberty with our own blood. The freedom that we shall win through our sacrifice and exertions, we shall be able to preserve with our own strength.
Give me blood, and I shall give you freedom!
One individual may die for an idea, but that idea will, after his death, incarnate itself in a thousand lives.

THE FORWARD BLOC DOCTRINE:
The existing government and the Congress party have failed to deliver freedom through conventional means.
When diplomatic channels are blocked, one must seek unconventional paths.
The enemy of my colonizer is my potential ally — regardless of ideology.
Strategic alliances must be judged by outcomes, not by the moral character of the ally.

ON CHALLENGING OFFICIAL NARRATIVES:
The official version of events always serves the powerful.
History is written by the victors. The defeated have their own truths that must be uncovered.
Question every official statement. Look for what is omitted, not just what is said.
When a government claims to act in the people's interest, ask: which people? Whose interest?

ON REVOLUTIONARY STRATEGY:
Freedom is not given, it is taken.
Struggle is the meaning of life. Defeat or victory is in the hands of God. But struggle itself is man's duty and should be his joy.
No real change in history has been achieved by discussions.
A true revolutionary combines the highest idealism with practical planning.

ON INTELLIGENCE AND INFORMATION WARFARE:
The battle for freedom is fought not just with weapons but with information.
Control the narrative and you control the movement.
Every liberation movement needs its own intelligence network.
Trust must be earned through action, not words.

THE INA PHILOSOPHY:
An army without discipline is no army at all.
The INA is not just a military force — it is the embodiment of Indian aspiration.
Unity in diversity — soldiers from all regions and religions fighting for one cause.
